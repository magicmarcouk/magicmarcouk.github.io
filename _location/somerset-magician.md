Marco can provide close-up, table and stand-up magic for Somerset weddings, parties, dinners and corporate events. His style is flexible enough for relaxed private celebrations, formal dinners and events where guests benefit from an easy way to start conversations.

Somerset is one of the areas Marco can cover from his London base, alongside regular bookings elsewhere in the UK and abroad.

## Magic for Somerset events

Close-up magic is a practical choice for venues where guests are spread across a reception, dining room or party space. Marco can perform for small groups, visit tables between courses or create a shared moment with a stand-up show when the event schedule allows.

This can work particularly well during drinks, after a meal or while photographs and room changes are taking place.

## Planning the performance

Because Somerset bookings may involve more travel planning, include the venue or postcode when you enquire. Marco can then confirm availability, travel arrangements and whether close-up, table or stand-up magic is the best fit.
