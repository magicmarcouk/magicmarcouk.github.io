Marco performs close-up, table and stand-up magic for Berkshire events, from private celebrations and weddings to corporate functions and client evenings. The performance can fit naturally into a drinks reception, dinner or party without needing guests to leave the flow of the event.

Berkshire is one of Marco's core service areas, and he also regularly takes bookings across the UK and abroad from his London base.

## Magic for Berkshire events

Berkshire has a wide range of event settings, including hotels, restaurants, offices, private homes and larger celebration venues. Marco can move between groups, entertain tables between courses or perform a stand-up routine when everyone is gathered together.

For company events, magic can help keep guests engaged during arrivals, networking or product launches. For weddings and family parties, it gives different age groups an easy shared moment.

## Planning the performance

When you contact Marco, include the venue or postcode, the event date, approximate numbers and any key timings such as drinks, dinner or speeches. He will help you choose the best point in the event for magic.
