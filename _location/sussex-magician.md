Marco can provide close-up, table and stand-up magic for Sussex weddings, private parties, dinners and corporate events. The performance is designed to fit around the event rather than pull guests away from it.

Sussex is one of the areas Marco can cover from his London base, alongside regular bookings elsewhere in the UK and abroad.

## Magic for Sussex venues

Close-up magic works well for drinks receptions, wedding breakfasts, private dinners, company parties and client events. Marco can move naturally between groups, entertain tables during dinner or present a stand-up show when the venue and timings suit a shared performance.

The format is useful for guests who have not met before, because it creates conversation without requiring a formal stage or complicated setup.

## Planning the performance

When you contact Marco, include the venue or postcode, event date, approximate guest numbers and the part of the event where entertainment would be most useful. He can then advise on timing, travel and format.
