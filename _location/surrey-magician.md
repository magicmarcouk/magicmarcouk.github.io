Marco provides close-up, table and stand-up magic for events across Surrey. His style works well for weddings, private parties, corporate dinners and celebrations where guests are gathered in smaller groups before or during a meal.

Surrey is one of Marco's core service areas, and he also regularly takes bookings across the UK and abroad from his London base.

## Magic for Surrey venues

Surrey events often bring together guests from different parts of London and the surrounding counties, so entertainment that breaks the ice can make a real difference. Marco can perform during drinks, visit tables between courses or add a short stand-up show after dinner.

Close-up magic is flexible for hotels, golf clubs, private homes, restaurants, village halls and corporate venues. It can be adapted to suit formal dinners, relaxed parties and family celebrations without taking over the event.

## Planning the performance

Useful enquiry details include the venue or town, the event date, the number of guests and whether you are considering reception magic, table magic or a short show. Marco can then advise on timing and format.
