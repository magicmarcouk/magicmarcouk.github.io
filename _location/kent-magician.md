Marco can provide close-up, table and stand-up magic for Kent weddings, parties, dinners and corporate events. His magic can be used as informal entertainment during a reception, table entertainment during dinner or a short show for everyone to enjoy together.

Kent is one of the areas Marco can cover from his London base, alongside regular bookings elsewhere in the UK and abroad.

## Magic for Kent events

For weddings and private parties, Marco can help guests mix during drinks, photographs or room changes. For corporate events, dinners and client evenings, close-up magic can keep people engaged between scheduled moments.

The performance can be adapted for hotels, restaurants, clubs, private homes and larger event spaces, with practical details agreed before the booking.

## Planning the performance

Include the venue or postcode, event date, guest numbers and likely running order when you enquire. Marco can then suggest the most suitable format and confirm travel arrangements.
