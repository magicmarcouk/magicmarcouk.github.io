Marco can provide close-up, table and stand-up magic for Wiltshire weddings, private parties, dinners and corporate events. His performance works well when guests are gathered for drinks, moving into dinner or meeting each other for the first time.

Wiltshire is one of the areas Marco can cover from his London base, alongside regular bookings elsewhere in the UK and abroad.

## Magic for Wiltshire occasions

Close-up magic can fit neatly into hotels, private homes, restaurants, clubs, marquees and celebration venues. Marco can move between groups during a reception, entertain tables between courses or present a short stand-up show when everyone is ready to watch together.

For weddings and family celebrations, the magic gives guests an easy shared talking point. For corporate dinners and client events, it helps keep the room engaged without interrupting the main schedule.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the venue or postcode, the event date, approximate guest numbers and the part of the day where entertainment would help most. Marco can then confirm availability, travel arrangements and the best performance format.
