Marco can provide close-up, table and stand-up magic for Hampshire weddings, parties, dinners and corporate events. For events where guests are arriving from different places or meeting for the first time, close-up magic helps create conversation quickly.

## Magic for Hampshire occasions

The performance can be arranged around the natural shape of the event. Marco can entertain during a drinks reception, visit tables during dinner or present a short stand-up show when the room is ready for everyone to watch together.

This flexibility is useful for hotels, private homes, restaurants, clubs and celebration venues. It also means the entertainment can work around photographs, food service, speeches and other scheduled moments.

## Planning the performance

Because Hampshire events may involve more travel planning, include the venue or postcode when you enquire. Marco can then confirm availability, travel arrangements and the most suitable performance format.
