Marco performs magic for London events where guests need entertainment that works around a busy schedule. Close-up magic is especially useful for drinks receptions, networking sessions, dinners and parties because it does not need a stage or a break in the event.

London is Marco's home base as well as one of his core service areas, and he also regularly takes bookings across the UK and abroad.

## Magic for London events

London venues vary enormously, from hotels and private dining rooms to offices, clubs and landmark event spaces. Marco can mingle with guests during arrivals, move between tables during dinner or present a stand-up show when the room and schedule suit a shared performance.

For corporate events, magic can help draw people into conversation during receptions, exhibitions and client evenings. For weddings and parties, it gives guests something memorable to share while photographs, food service or room changes are taking place.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the venue or postcode, the event date, approximate guest numbers and the part of the event where entertainment would help most. Marco will suggest whether close-up, table or stand-up magic is the best fit.
