Marco can provide close-up, table and stand-up magic for Oxfordshire weddings, private parties, dinners and corporate events. His performance can sit naturally within a reception, dinner or celebration without needing a formal stage.

Oxfordshire is one of the areas Marco can cover from his London base, alongside regular bookings elsewhere in the UK and abroad.

## Magic for Oxfordshire occasions

Close-up magic is well suited to drinks receptions, wedding breakfasts, corporate dinners, private parties and client events. Marco can move between guests, entertain tables between courses or present a stand-up show when everyone is gathered together.

For events with guests arriving from different places, magic helps create a shared experience early in the day or evening.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the venue or postcode, event date, approximate guest numbers and any key timings. Marco can then advise on travel, format and where the magic will fit best.
