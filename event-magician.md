Close-up magic helps guests relax, break the ice and start conversations. It can work equally well at a charity ball, awards dinner, private dinner, club event or celebration where some guests are meeting for the first time.

## Choose a format

Marco can perform in one style or combine several to suit the venue and schedule:

- **Close-up magic** — performed for small groups during drinks or networking.
- **Table magic** — entertainment between courses for guests awaiting service.
- **Stand-up magic** — a shared show for the audience to watch together.

At dinners, Marco can mingle during pre-dinner drinks before moving between tables in the main room. He judges when to approach each group so that the performance works around conversation and service.

Marco's event rate usually covers up to two hours, which is generally enough time to engage with most guests without taking the emphasis away from the main event. A stand-up show can last from 10 minutes to an hour and can be performed for up to 80 people without a microphone or stage, or for a larger audience when suitable equipment is available.

## Straightforward preparation

Marco arrives ready to perform and does not need a changing area. He will discuss any requirements for a stand-up show with you in advance.
