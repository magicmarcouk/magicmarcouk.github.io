Charity events often need entertainment that keeps guests engaged while still leaving room for fundraising, speeches, auctions and the purpose of the evening. Marco's close-up magic can help guests relax, mix and stay involved between the important moments.

## Magic for fundraising events

Marco can perform during a drinks reception, move between tables during a meal or present a short stand-up show when the room is ready for a shared performance. The format can be adapted for charity balls, fundraising dinners, auctions, receptions and private donor events.

Magic can also create natural talking points during quieter parts of the schedule, helping guests connect without taking attention away from the cause.

## Marco's charity story

Marco has a personal connection with charity fundraising through the signed playing-card poster donated to Comic Relief for a Red Nose Day auction. You can read the story on the [charity poster]({{ '/charity-poster/' | relative_url }}) page.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the venue, event format, guest numbers, running order and any fundraising moments that need clear space. Marco can advise on the best timing for close-up, table or stand-up magic.
