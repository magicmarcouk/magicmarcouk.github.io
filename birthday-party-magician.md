Marco's close-up magic can add energy to a birthday party without taking over the celebration. It works well for adult birthdays, milestone parties and family gatherings where guests are mixing in small groups.

## Magic for milestone birthdays

Marco can mingle during drinks, perform around tables during a meal or present a stand-up show for everyone at once. The format can be adapted for a private home, restaurant, hotel, garden party or hired venue.

For a milestone birthday, Marco can discuss personal touches such as incorporating a photograph into a playing-card trick. The signed card can become a souvenir for the guest of honour.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the party date, venue or postcode, approximate guest numbers and whether the event includes drinks, dinner or a moment when everyone will be together.
