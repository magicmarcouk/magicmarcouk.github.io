Christmas parties often bring together colleagues, clients, friends or family members who do not all know each other well. Marco's close-up magic helps break the ice and gives guests something memorable to share.

## Magic for festive events

Marco can perform during welcome drinks, visit tables during a festive dinner or present a short stand-up show when everyone is gathered together. The performance can fit around food service, speeches, awards, music and dancing.

For company Christmas parties, close-up magic can keep the room lively before dinner or between scheduled moments. For private festive celebrations, it gives guests a shared experience without needing a formal stage.

## Planning the performance

December dates can be busy, so enquire as early as possible. Include the venue, date, guest numbers and running order so Marco can advise on the best format and timing.
