Corporate dinners need entertainment that keeps the room lively without disrupting the meal, speeches or awards. Marco can perform close-up magic during drinks, table magic between courses or a short stand-up show when guests are gathered together.

## Magic for dinners and awards nights

At a formal dinner, Marco can mingle during the reception before moving between tables once guests are seated. He judges when to approach each group so the magic works around conversation, service and the evening's running order.

For awards nights, gala dinners and client evenings, close-up magic gives guests a shared experience without requiring a stage or complicated setup. A short stand-up routine can also work well when the room is ready for everyone to watch together. If the awards are the main focus of the evening, see the dedicated [awards night magic]({{ '/awards-night-magician/' | relative_url }}) page.

## Planning the performance

Useful details include the venue, dinner timings, guest numbers, table layout and whether speeches or awards are part of the evening. Marco can then suggest the best point for close-up, table or stand-up magic.
