At an exhibition or trade show, the first challenge is getting people to stop. Marco's close-up magic can attract attention, create a natural reason for visitors to stay at the stand and give your team an easy way to begin a conversation.

## Magic for exhibition stands

Marco can perform short, visual routines for small groups throughout the day. The magic is designed to work in busy spaces without a stage, microphone or long setup, so it can fit around visitor flow and sales conversations.

For product launches and campaign events, Marco can discuss ways to connect the magic to a company logo, product or message. The aim is not just to entertain, but to make the stand more memorable.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the exhibition location, stand size, event hours and the kind of visitor interaction you want to encourage. Marco can then advise on timings, breaks and whether any branded magic would suit the event.
