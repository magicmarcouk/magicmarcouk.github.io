A bar mitzvah celebration brings together family, friends and guests of different ages. Marco's close-up magic can help guests mix, create shared reactions and add entertainment that works around the party schedule.

## Magic for the celebration

Marco can perform during arrivals, mingle between groups, visit tables during food service or present a stand-up show when everyone is gathered together. The format can be adapted for a hotel, private venue, restaurant, hall or family celebration.

Close-up magic works particularly well when guests are moving between drinks, food, music and speeches, because it does not need a formal stage or a long setup.

## Planning the performance

When you enquire, include the venue, date, approximate guest numbers, age range and running order. Marco can then advise on the best timing and performance style for the celebration.
