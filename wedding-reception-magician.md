A wedding reception is one of the best moments for close-up magic. Guests are arriving, photographs may be taking place and different groups are beginning to meet. Marco can move naturally through the room, giving people something shared to laugh about and remember.

## Magic during the reception

Marco can perform during welcome drinks, while photographs are being taken, between parts of the day or as evening guests arrive. The magic works in small groups, so it does not interrupt the flow of the reception or require everyone to stop what they are doing.

This can be especially useful when there is a gap between the ceremony and the wedding breakfast, or when day and evening guests are mixing for the first time.

## A personal touch

Marco can discuss personalising a trick for the couple, such as making a photograph of the newlyweds appear on a playing card chosen, inspected and signed by a guest. The signed card can become a small keepsake from the day.
