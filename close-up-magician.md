Close-up magic is Marco's most flexible style of performance. It happens right in front of guests, usually with cards, borrowed objects and plenty of interaction, so people can watch, react and join in together.

Marco is based in London and regularly performs close-up magic at weddings, private parties, corporate events and special occasions across the UK and abroad.

## When does close-up magic work best?

Close-up magic is ideal when guests are already gathered in small groups or moving naturally around a venue. Marco can perform during:

- drinks receptions;
- wedding photographs;
- corporate networking;
- exhibition stand conversations;
- private parties;
- table-to-table entertainment;
- charity events, awards nights and seasonal celebrations.

Because the performance happens among the guests, it does not need a stage, microphone or formal audience setup. Marco can work around food service, speeches, photographs and other planned moments.

## Close-up, table and stand-up magic

For many events, close-up magic is enough on its own. For dinners or larger celebrations, Marco can combine it with table magic or a short stand-up show so the entertainment fits the shape of the day or evening.

If you are unsure which format is right, Marco can suggest the best approach once he knows the venue, guest numbers and running order.
