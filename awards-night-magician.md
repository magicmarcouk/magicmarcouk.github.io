Awards nights need entertainment that keeps the energy up while respecting the ceremony, speeches and presentations. Marco can perform close-up magic during arrivals, table magic around the seated parts of the evening or a short stand-up show when the schedule allows.

## Magic around the running order

Before the ceremony, close-up magic can help guests settle in and start conversations. During seated sections, table magic can add entertainment without requiring a stage change. After the awards, Marco can help keep the celebration lively as guests continue the evening.

This makes awards-night magic useful for company awards, industry ceremonies, gala evenings, association events and staff celebrations.

## Planning the performance

Useful details include the venue, guest numbers, table plan, ceremony timings and whether there are speeches, presentations or entertainment already scheduled. Marco can then suggest where magic will add the most value without competing with the awards.
